## 1. Getting Started

### Sign In with Your Phone Number

Shalom uses **phone number verification** (OTP) to keep your account secure. No passwords to remember.

1. Open [shalomapp.in](https://shalomapp.in) in your browser.
2. You'll see the **Shalom splash screen** — it redirects to the sign-in page after a few seconds.
3. Enter your **phone number** (Indian format, e.g., `9876543210` — the app adds `+91` automatically).
4. Tap **Send OTP** — you'll receive a 6-digit code via SMS.
5. Enter the OTP and tap **Verify**.
6. You're in!

> **Note:** You must be a registered member of a church on Shalom to sign in. If your number isn't found, contact your church admin to add you.

### First-Time Users

When you sign in for the first time, you'll see an **onboarding guide** on your dashboard:

1. **Complete your profile** — add your name, photo, address, and date of birth.
2. **Wait for your admin** — your church administrator will assign subscriptions (offerings, tithes, etc.) to you.
3. **Pay when due** — once subscriptions are assigned, you'll see them on your dashboard.

## 2. Exploring & Joining a Church

The **Explore** page is public — no sign-in required.

### Search for a Church

1. Visit the Explore page.
2. Type your church's name in the search bar.
3. If found, you'll see the church's **name, address, and location**.
4. Tap **Sign In** to join (your church admin must have already added your phone number as a member).

### Church Not Listed?

If your church isn't on Shalom yet, the Explore page provides a way to **request a listing** — your church leadership can reach out to get onboarded.

### What Shalom Offers

- Pay offerings, tithes, and subscriptions online
- Get alerts on upcoming programs and events
- Receive birthday and anniversary greetings from your church
- Multi-language support (English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada)
- Track and pay subscriptions (including family members)
- Instant digital receipts for all payments
- Secure profile management

**For Churches:**
- Digital transformation for your congregation
- One-click member profile management
- Reach your entire congregation with announcements
- Financial dashboards and reports
- Excel member export
- Auto-generated payment receipts
- Single-click communications via push notifications and SMS

## 3. Your Dashboard

After signing in, the **Dashboard** is your home screen. It's organized as a clean bento-grid layout.

### Dues & Contributions

This card shows:

- **Total Outstanding Balance** — the total amount you owe across all subscriptions.
- **Active Subscriptions** — each subscription shows its name, amount, billing cycle (monthly/quarterly/yearly), status, and next due date.
- **Family Member Dues** — if you have linked family members, their dues appear here with a **"Family"** tag. You can pay for them too.

**To pay:**
1. Check the boxes next to the dues you want to pay.
2. Tap **Pay Now**.
3. Review the **Payment Summary** (itemized breakdown).
4. Complete payment via Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking, wallets).

**To request cancellation of a subscription:**
1. Tap the **Request Cancellation** button on the subscription.
2. Enter a reason.
3. Your church admin will review and approve/reject the request.

### Quick Actions

- **Prayer Request** — jump to the prayer request page.
- **Donate** — go to the donation page.

### Latest Announcements

Shows the **3 most recent** church announcements with title, message, and how long ago they were posted (e.g., "2 hours ago").

### Upcoming Events

Shows up to **4 upcoming events** with a calendar-style date block (month/day), title, and a message preview.

## 5. Family Members

Link your family members to your profile so you can manage and pay their subscriptions together.

### Adding a Family Member

1. Go to your **Profile** page → **Family Members** section.
2. **Search by name** — type a family member's name to search existing church members.
3. Select the person from the results.
4. Choose the **relationship**: Spouse, Father, Mother, Son, Daughter, Brother, Sister, Grandfather, Grandmother, Uncle, Aunt, Nephew, Niece, Cousin, or Other.
5. Submit the **family link request**.

> **Important:** Family link requests require **admin approval**. Your admin will review and approve/reject the request.

### Managing Family Members

- **Edit**: Update a family member's name, gender, relation, age, or date of birth.
- **Remove**: Remove a family member link (confirmation required).

### Family Requests

Check the **Family Requests Panel** on your profile to see the status of your requests:
- **Pending** — waiting for admin review.
- **Approved** — linked successfully.
- **Rejected** — declined (review notes from admin will be shown).

## 6. Special Dates (Birthdays & Anniversaries)

Your church can send you greetings on special occasions! Add your important dates.

## 7. Subscriptions & Dues

Your church admin assigns subscriptions to you (e.g., monthly offerings, annual tithes, building fund contributions). These appear on your **Dashboard**.

Each subscription shows:
- **Name** (e.g., "Monthly Offering", "Building Fund")

- **Billing Cycle** — Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly
- **Status** — Active, Overdue, etc.
- **Next Due Date**

### Paying Subscriptions

1. On your Dashboard, check the subscriptions you want to pay.
2. Tap **Pay Now**.
3. Review the itemized summary.
4. Complete payment through Razorpay.

### Family Subscriptions

If you have linked family members with assigned subscriptions, their dues appear alongside yours with a **"Family"** tag. You can select and pay them together in a single transaction.

### Cancellation Requests

If you need to cancel a subscription:
1. Tap **Request Cancellation** on the subscription.
2. Provide a reason.
3. The admin reviews your request — you'll be notified of the outcome.

## 8. Making Payments

All payments are processed securely through **Razorpay**, India's leading payment gateway.

### Supported Payment Methods

- **UPI** (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
- **Debit/Credit Cards** (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- **Net Banking**
- **Wallets**

### Payment Flow

1. Select what you want to pay (subscriptions, donations, etc.).
2. Review the amount and details.
3. Tap **Pay Now** — the Razorpay checkout opens.
4. Choose your payment method and complete the transaction.
5. You'll receive an **instant digital receipt**.

### Payment Security

- All transactions are encrypted and processed by Razorpay.
- Payment data is verified using HMAC signatures.
- Duplicate payments are automatically detected and prevented.
- Your card/bank details are never stored by Shalom.

## 9. Donations

Make one-time donations to your church — available to **both members and anonymous visitors**.

### How to Donate

1. Navigate to the **Donate** page (from Dashboard or Explore page).
2. **Choose an amount**: Quick-select buttons (₹100, ₹500, ₹1,000, ₹2,500, ₹5,000, ₹10,000) or enter a **custom amount**.
3. **Select a fund**: General Offering, Building Fund, Mission & Outreach, Youth Ministry, Community Aid, or Other. (Your church may have custom fund names.)
4. **Your info** (optional): Add your name, email, and a personal message.
5. Tap **Continue to Payment** — complete via Razorpay.

### Anonymous Donations

You don't need to be signed in to donate. Anonymous visitors can donate by providing optional contact info.

## 10. Prayer Requests

Send prayer requests directly to your church's pastoral leadership.

### Sending a Prayer Request

1. Go to the **Prayer Request** page.
2. You'll see a grid of your church's leaders:
   - **District Chairperson**
   - **Presbyter**
   - **Pastor**
   - Each card shows their photo, role, name, and bio.

   - Or tap **"General Request"** to send to all leaders at once.

6. Tap **Submit**.
7. You'll see a **confirmation screen** with options to go back to the Dashboard or send another request.

### Viewing Your Prayer History

At the bottom of the Prayer Request page, you can see your **previous requests** with:
- Status badges (submitted, reviewed, etc.)
- Timestamps

## 14. Installing the App (PWA)

Shalom is a **Progressive Web App** — you can install it on your phone's home screen for an app-like experience, no app store needed.

### On Android (Chrome)

1. Open [shalomapp.in](https://shalomapp.in) in Chrome.
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right.
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**.
4. Confirm — the Shalom icon appears on your home screen.

### On iPhone (Safari)

1. Open [shalomapp.in](https://shalomapp.in) in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **Add** — the Shalom icon appears on your home screen.

### On Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

1. Open [shalomapp.in](https://shalomapp.in).
2. Click the **install icon** (⊕) in the address bar.
3. Click **Install**.

### Offline Support

The app caches essential resources. If you lose internet connectivity, an **offline indicator** appears and you can still view cached content. Full functionality resumes when you're back online.

## 15. For Church Admins

Church admins have access to additional management tabs on the Dashboard.

### Member Management

- **Search members** by name, email, or phone number.
- **View member details**: Full profile, membership ID, verification status.
- **Update members**: Edit name and change verification status (Pending → Verified → Rejected → Suspended).
- **Delete members**: Preview the impact first (how many family links, subscriptions, and payments will be affected), then confirm by typing a keyword.

### Subscription Management

- Create and assign subscriptions to members.
- Set billing cycles (monthly, quarterly, yearly) and amounts.
- Review and approve/reject cancellation requests from members.

### Income Dashboard

Track your church's finances at a glance:

| Metric | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Today's Income** | Collections received today |
| **This Month** | Monthly running total |
| **This Year** | Yearly running total |
| **Total Payments** | Count of all payment transactions |

**Charts:**
- **Weekly bar chart** — subscription and donation income by day.
- **Monthly trend chart** — income trends over months.

**Reports:**
- Download **CSV reports** for any period:
  - Today
  - Monthly (select year & month)
  - Yearly (select year)
  - Custom date range
- Reports include full transaction details.

### Events & Announcements

- Create church events with title, description, date, and optional images.
- Post announcements/notifications to your congregation.
- Events and announcements appear on members' dashboards and Events page.

### Push Notifications & SMS

Send messages to your congregation:

1. **Choose channel**: Push Notification or SMS.
2. **Select audience**: All members, specific diocese, specific church, or individual member.
3. **Compose message**:
   - **Push**: Title + message (up to 1,000 characters) + landing page (Home, Dashboard, etc.).
   - **SMS**: Message only (up to 320 characters).
4. Tap **Send**.

**Batch Tracking:**
- View recent notification batches with status (Sending/Completed).
- See counts: total, sent, failed, cancelled.
- Auto-refreshes while sending.
- Cancel in-progress batches if needed.
- Expand any batch to see details.

### Bulk Member Import

Add many members at once using a CSV file:

1. Prepare a CSV file with columns: `full_name`, `email`, `phone_number`, `address`.
2. Upload the file (up to 500 rows).
3. Preview the import — see how many members will be added.
4. Tap **Import Members**.
5. Review results — each row shows: Created, Skipped (duplicate), or Failed (with error details).

> Phone numbers are automatically normalized to Indian format (+91).

### Family Request Approvals

Review and approve/reject family link requests from members. Add optional review notes explaining your decision.

## 17. Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q: I can't sign in. What should I do?**
A: You must be registered as a member by your church admin. Contact your church administrator and provide your phone number so they can add you.

**Q: Can I sign in with email or Google?**
A: No. Shalom uses phone number + OTP verification only for security.

**Q: I changed my phone number. How do I update it?**
A: Sign in with your current number, go to Profile, edit your phone number, and verify the new number via OTP.

**Q: Can I be a member of multiple churches?**
A: Currently, each member is associated with one church.

### Payments

**Q: Is it safe to pay online?**
A: Yes. All payments go through Razorpay, India's leading payment gateway. Your card/bank details are never stored by Shalom.

**Q: I was charged twice for the same payment.**
A: Shalom has duplicate payment detection built in. If you believe you were double-charged, check your History page. Contact your church admin with the receipt number.

**Q: Can I get a receipt for my payment?**
A: Yes. Every payment generates a downloadable PDF receipt. Go to History and tap **Download Receipt** next to any transaction.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and digital wallets — all via Razorpay.

**Q: Can I pay for my family members?**
A: Yes. Once family members are linked and approved, their subscriptions appear on your Dashboard with a "Family" tag. Select them and pay together.

### Subscriptions

**Q: How do I get subscriptions assigned to me?**
A: Your church admin assigns subscriptions. Once assigned, they appear on your Dashboard.

**Q: Can I cancel a subscription?**
A: You can request cancellation. Tap "Request Cancellation" on the subscription card and provide a reason. Your admin will review it.

### Family

**Q: How do I add a family member?**
A: Go to Profile → Family Members → search for their name → select relationship → submit. Admin approval is required.

**Q: My family member isn't showing in search results.**
A: They must first be registered as a church member by your admin. Ask your admin to add them.

### General

**Q: What languages does the app support?**
A: English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**
A: Basic caching is available. You can view previously loaded content offline, but actions like payments and form submissions require an internet connection.

**Q: Is there a mobile app on the App Store / Play Store?**
A: Shalom is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it directly from your browser — see [Installing the App](#14-installing-the-app-pwa). No app store download needed.

## 18. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| **OTP not received** | Wait 30 seconds and try again. Ensure your phone number is correct (+91 format). Check if SMS is blocked by your carrier. |
| **Page not loading** | Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page. Clear browser cache if the issue persists. |
| **Push notifications not working** | Go to Settings → Push Notifications and ensure they're enabled. Check your browser's notification permissions. On iOS, add the app to your home screen first. |
| **Payment failed** | Check your bank/UPI app for the transaction status. If money was deducted but payment shows as pending, wait 15 minutes — it usually auto-resolves. Contact your admin with the transaction ID. |
| **Can't see family member dues** | Ensure the family link request is approved (check Profile → Family Requests). The family member must have active subscriptions assigned by the admin. |
| **App looks outdated** | The app auto-updates when a new version is deployed. Force-refresh with Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac). |
| **Dark mode colors look wrong** | Toggle dark mode off and on in Settings. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache. |
| **Downloaded receipt is blank** | Ensure pop-ups are allowed for shalomapp.in. Try a different browser. |
